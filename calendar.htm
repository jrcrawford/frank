Wednesday, April 12, 2006 at 10:00 AM Duration: 60 Minutes
Moderator
Arvid Mortensen & Charles Lee

Subject

Please join Arvid Mortensen MBA, JD, CLU, AEP and Charles Lee, Life Sales and Marketing
Manager to discuss creative uses of Irrevocable Life Insurance Trusts. Life insurance products
have become more competitive over the last 5 years as mortality costs have dropped. Sharing
this great sales idea with your valued clients may create a real opportunity to do something
nice for both your clients and you. Please consider your sales prospects and current clients
and ask yourself whether you should participate in this web conference. Sales prospects
and current clients who meet the following criteria are good candidates for this sales idea:

  • Those who want to keep assets from federal estate taxation
  • Those who want to maximize the money spent on life insurance
  • Those who have an asset base that may be expected to increase
    over the next 10 years or longer
  • Those who have existing life insurance policies with face amounts over $50,000
  • Those who would like to use a sophisticated, precise estate planning method
  • Those who are easy to work with and accept advice well

If you have prospects or clients that fit any of these parameters, please join us for this web
conference to learn how your client and you can benefit from this great sales idea.
