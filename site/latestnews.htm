Now – Vital UL Is Now Part Of The CPS Website. Vital UL is a universal life rate spreadsheet program you can use to quickly identify the most competitive products (from the carriers participating in the program) for your clients looking at permanent coverage. Vital UL is now a permanent part of the Vital Term / Vital Signs package already available to you at the CPS website – the same ID and password you use to access Vital Term / Vital Signs gets you access to Vital UL. Vital UL is to be used for rate comparison purposes only – a complete illustration must be run using the specific carriers’ software program or winflex web, and is always required with any UL application submission. Contact your CPS Sales Rep for more information.

Now - Latest Issues Of Stephan Leimberg's Activator, Now Available. Now - Sell Mutual Of Omaha's LTC To Earn Chances To Win A Mercedes. Just five LTC apps get you in the drawing. The grand prize is two years lease on a Mercedes CLK320 Cabriolet, other prizes are: technology packages (Nokia 7700 or similar current model at time of drawing), leather golf bags, and promotional items. Now – You Can Order Your Client’s Exam. Need to “get the ball rolling”? On the occasion you would like to order your client’s exam, in advance of submitting the application to CPS - click on the Order Your Own Exam menu item at the CPS website to connect to Superior Mobile Medics’ website - you may complete the process of ordering the required exam for your client on your own. Be reminded - if you order an exam and an application for the client is never submitted, you will be responsible for the cost of the exam (you’ll be billed by the vendor). Important - any time you order the exam prior to submitting an app (whether you use Superior Mobile Medics or not), please remember to: 1) Let the vendor know to send the exam to CPS – don’t have it sent directly to the carrier, and 2) Include a cover letter with the app – let us know you have ordered the exam yourself (and let us know which vendor you ordered it from). Any issues or questions in this regard, please contact your RFG Case Manager.

Now – Sales Tip: Offers Not As Applied For – You Can Help Us Expedite The Process Of Getting Your Client Insured. The Informal Inquiry Form or Informal Inquiry 15-Point Type (version required in CA) must be completed, signed and submitted to CPS any time a case needs to be shopped (an offer comes back not as applied for, the case can not be placed with the initial carrier selected, and another carrier must be found). To help avoid delays in the issuance of a policy to your client, you should make a point of taking a copy of the Informal Inquiry form with you any time you are taking an app - get it completed and signed, and send it in. We’ll keep it on file - you never know when it may be needed.
