Individual Agent Coverage

Serving the needs of insurance agents and others engaged in insurance and related financial services is NAIFA's mission. The NAIFA-endorsed Professional Liability (E&O) Program is designed to meet your unique needs. Coverage is underwritten by New Hampshire Insurance Company, a member of American Insurance Group, Inc. (AIG), the world’s leading international insurance and financial services organization, with operations in more than 130 countries and jurisdictions.

The NAIFA-endorsed Professional Liability Insurance Program is only available to NAIFA members. If you are not a NAIFA member, learn about NAIFA.

Errors & Omissions Coverage
24-hour protection against claims arising from negligent acts, errors and omissions in the conduct of your business.

Choice of Limits
Limits include both a per claim limit and a higher annual aggregate limit.
  • $250,000/ $500,000
  • $500,000/ $1,000,000
  • $1,000,000/ $2,000,000*
  • $2,000,000/ $2,000,000
*The highest limit available in the states of Alabama and Texas is $1,000,000/$2,000,000.

Choice of Deductibles
  • $1,000
  • $2,500
  • $5,000
Claim Expenses Outside the Limits
Your complete legal defense is paid in addition to the limits of liability you choose, for all covered claims. Regardless of whether you win or lose or even if the claim or suit is found groundless, false or fraudulent, your legal defense will be paid, subject to the "consent to settle" provisions of the policy. In certain circumstances, you may be offered coverage which applies the deductible to defense costs.

Protection for Carrier Insolvency
As long as the products you sell are fully insured by an admitted carrier and rated A- or better by A. M. Best at the time of placement, you're protected against legal liability as a result of future insolvency of that carrier.

Wrap-Around Coverage The policy can provide coverage around your existing errors and omission coverage. Your present program may provide coverage for certain products. The NAIFA-endorsed program can 'wrap' around the existing coverage to provide coverage for activities not covered under the existing E&O policy. (This is subject to underwriting approval.)

Protection Against Personal Injury Lawsuits
Protects you against personal injury lawsuits resulting from slander, libel, invasion of privacy or false arrest while performing professional services as a life insurance agent or broker.

Coverage for You as an Individual Agent
Coverage is available for you as a life/ accident/ health agent, general agent or broker and includes your non-producing clerical employees.
