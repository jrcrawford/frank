Even in a low interest rate environment, Return of Premium Term (ROP) is still one of the best guaranteed values for Life Insurance on the market today.

"As many of you know, claims experience for Term Insurance equates to less that 3%. Yet clients still pay premiums for 10, 15, 20 and 30 years with a less than 3% chance there will be a claim paid. Premium payments on Term policies that never pay a claim represent pure cost to our clients and profit to the insurance carriers.

We have all heard the phrase, buy Term and invest the difference. But what can clients really invest in today that has some form of guarantee and could be meaningful to them without a large sum of cash?

How does 4.02% guaranteed rate of return on as little as $565 a year sound?

Assume a 35- year- old male client purchased a $500,000 30 year Term policy to protect his family and mortgage. His premium at preferred rates would be around $538 annually for 30 years. A Return of Premium Term policy for the under the same profile would cost $1,103. That is a premium difference of $565.

At the end of 30 years the total pure cost to the insured is $16,140 under the traditional Term policy. But for as little as an additional $565 a year the client could have a guaranteed payout of $33,090 and still be alive to enjoy it. That is a guaranteed return of 4.02% for the next 30 years on $565.

The producer was able to double commissionable sale and provide the client with a policy that protects his family and has significant value at the end of the term.
