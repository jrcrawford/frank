The Power Of 3, The Power Of Exam-Free

Exam-Free Term Upgrade
Did you know……half of U.S. households admit they currently don’t have enough Life Insurance protection (Source: LIMRA U.S. Marketplace newsletter, Vol. 17, Number 3, 2010)? Chances are you have clients who need more insurance, but at today’s hectic pace it’s hard to make time for the medical tests required by most carriers. Now, American General can help busy families close the coverage gap the easy way, with the AG Exam-Free Term UpgradeSM.

In-force clients who have been medically underwritten for level premium Term Insurance within the last five years may be eligible for the AG Exam-Free Term Upgrade. Prior coverage from nearly all major carriers is eligible and no blood work or lab tests are required. AG Exam-Free Term Upgrade offers three times the original coverage, up to $500,000.

The Power of 3%
Just a few years ago, you would have been laughed at for suggesting that a 3% yield was competitive. However, with 10 year treasury bonds today trading below 2.7%, even the dividend yield on many high quality stocks is higher than this and suddenly 3% seems like a good rate.

And as far as corporate bonds go, demand there remains incredibly frothy as well. In fact, Johnson and Johnson just recently made history by issuing the lowest rate ever on a 10 year corporate bond, at 2.95%. (Source: MotleyFool.com, 9/5/10). Again, 3% now seems like a pretty decent fixed yield. But where can you get a yield like this, and perhaps even more?

Fortunately, for your affluent clients who need more protection for their families as well as cash accumulation potential, there is a solution. There is a Life Insurance product today that offers your clients the ability to leverage the potential growth of the world’s major economies, while guaranteeing them in the worst case a minimum guaranteed lifetime interest rate of 3.00%.

That product is called the Elite Global Plus® Index Universal Life Insurance policy and is one of a very rare breed of Indexed Universal Life Insurance policies that provide exposure to multiple indices. Talk to your American General Regional Vice President or Internal Wholesaler today for more information on this unique product.

WCL's IRA Planning Strategies

Estate taxes have gotten a lot of attention in recent years, but what about the effect income taxes have on your client’s assets at death? West Coast Life focuses a lot of energy on developing sales concepts and strategies that are relevant to your client’s successful transfer of wealth. With that in mind, we are pleased to introduce West Coast Life’s 'Putting Your IRA to Work' strategy.

Our comprehensive marketing kit (available on line) is designed to help you present several different IRA planning strategies that may dramatically increase the value of your clients’ IRAs to beneficiaries. The three main strategies discussed are as follows:

  1.  Income Tax Offset – This strategy uses Life Insurance to offset the projected income taxes beneficiaries would incur if they chose to take a lump sum settlement of the IRA inheritance.
  2. Income Tax Elimination – For clients with charitable interests, this strategy uses Life Insurance to help reduce any income taxes that might be associated with a beneficiary inheriting the IRA, while providing a death benefit to the beneficiary that is equal to the peak value of the IRA.
  3.  Enhance Inheritance – This strategy appeals to clients who want to enhance the amount they transfer to their loved ones. With this approach, clients would withdraw the estimated annual earnings from the IRA and pay taxes on that amount. They would use the after-tax annual withdrawal amount to purchase continuous pay Life Insurance and name their loved ones as beneficiaries of the life policy and the IRA.
